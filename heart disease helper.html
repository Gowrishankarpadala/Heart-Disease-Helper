Welcome

Heart disease is the leading cause of death worldwide. Learn about symptoms, causes, and prevention tips to protect your heart.

Symptoms

  • Chest Pain
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Fatigue
  • Left Hand pain

Causes

  • Poor diet
  • Smoking
  • High blood pressure
  • Lack of exercise

Remedies

  • Eat a heart-healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Quit smoking
  • Manage stress effectively
  • Get regular health checkups

Action to perform incase of Heart attack

  • Start CPR if the person Becomes Unconscious
  • Call Emergency Services(ph no:112/911)
  • Keep the person calm and Seated
  • Give Aspirin if Available
